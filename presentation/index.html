03 — Our Solution

Digital Experience
Recovery Agent

Five AI agents that detect, diagnose, and coordinate recovery for digital banking incidents — while humans approve every high-risk action.

Hermes acts as the agentic runtime layer powering supervisor orchestration, sub-agent delegation, session control and approval-aware reasoning. The frontend is a recovery cockpit built around that runtime.

Detect

Diagnose

09 — Risk & Compliance

Safe by Design

The AI gives a wrong answer
Every finding must include a real ID, error code, or timestamp. If unsure, it sends the question to a human. It never guesses.
The AI acts without permission
The system always stops before taking action. The rollback command is written but never runs without a human pressing Authorize. In the MVP and target governance design, high-risk actions require explicit human approval.
Customer data is exposed
Before any transcript reaches an agent, an automatic filter removes all names, account numbers, and card details.
